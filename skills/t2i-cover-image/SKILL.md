# t2i-cover-image

Purpose
- Generate high-impact cover images for articles, posts, and reports.

Options
- `cover_type`: `hero`, `conceptual`, `typography`, `metaphor`, `scene`, `minimal`
- `palette`: `warm`, `elegant`, `cool`, `dark`, `earth`, `vivid`, `pastel`, `mono`, `retro`, `duotone`, `macaron`
- `rendering`: `flat-vector`, `hand-drawn`, `painterly`, `digital`, `pixel`, `chalk`, `screen-print`
- `text_level`: `none`, `title-only`, `title-subtitle`, `text-rich`
- `mood`: `subtle`, `balanced`, `bold`
- `font`: `clean`, `handwritten`, `serif`, `display`
- `lang`: `auto`, `zh`, `en`, `ja`

Guidelines
- One strong visual metaphor per cover.
- Prioritize title readability and visual focus.
- Keep composition clean with a clear focal point.
- Avoid busy backgrounds that reduce legibility.

Deliverables
- Analysis: derive key hook, audience tone, and visual metaphor.
- Outline: one cover concept per image.
- Prompt: include composition, typography area, and color direction.

Quality bar
- No watermark, logo, or random corner text.
- Strong contrast between foreground and background.
- Text-safe area is explicitly reserved.
