# t2i-diagram

Purpose
- Turn source content into structured, publication-ready diagram images.

Diagram Types (`layout`)
- `architecture`
- `flowchart`
- `sequence`
- `structural`
- `mind-map`
- `timeline`
- `illustrative`
- `state-machine`
- `data-flow`

Guidelines
- Focus on clarity, hierarchy, and visual grouping.
- Prefer concise labels and explicit directional flow.
- Use consistent icon style and spacing.
- Keep text readable at mobile size.

Deliverables
- Analysis: identify key entities, relationships, and flow.
- Outline: one image per major diagram slice.
- Prompt: include node groups, connectors, legend rules, and typography constraints.

Quality bar
- No cluttered overlaps.
- No watermark, logo, or random corner text.
- Preserve factual alignment with source.
